# Bug Audit Memo — 2026-03-30

## Observed pre-correction behavior
- `_refundEmployer(...)` enforced burn via `burnFrom(job.employer, burnAmount)`.
- Employer-refund terminal paths reached `_refundEmployer(...)` from:
  - `finalizeJob` when disapprovals > approvals after review window.
  - `resolveDisputeWithCode(..., resolutionCode=2)` (employer win).
  - `resolveStaleDispute(..., employerWins=true)`.

## Successful completion paths
- `_completeJob(...)` from:
  - `finalizeJob` success branches.
  - `resolveDisputeWithCode(..., resolutionCode=1)`.
  - `resolveStaleDispute(..., employerWins=false)`.

## Semantic mismatch
- Burn executed on employer-refund outcomes, not completion-only outcomes.
- Prior docs/release messaging described production-ready employer-burn semantics but did not reflect corrected completion-only requirement.

## Liveness risk in naive fix
- Moving direct `burnFrom(employer, amount)` into completion settlement creates employer allowance/balance griefing risk and can block valid successful completion finalization.
